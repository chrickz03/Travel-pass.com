Pantabangan Dam

Is an earth-fill embankment dam on the Pampanga River located in Pantabangan in Nueva Ecija province of the Philippines. The multi-purpose dam provides water for irrigation and hydroelectric power generation while its reservoir, Pantabangan Lake, affords flood control.

Location: Pantabangan in Nueva Ecija province of the Philippines

Ridge by Desmond Farm

Take a well-deserved break and reconnect with nature!
